About Us

Established in 1989, Flavorite Foods is a leading name in the Indian spice market. Founded by Mrs. Seeja PC, our company has grown into a full-fledged manufacturer and distributor of blended masalas and powders. Committed to authenticity and innovation, we offer a wide range of products, including regional cuisine favorites like rice powders, beverages, pickles, and ready-to-eat meals.Our raw materials are sourced from trusted suppliers in Pollachi and Guntur, ensuring the finest quality for our products. We are dedicated to supporting local employment and contributing to the well-being of the community. Positioned for growth and success, Flavorite Foods invites you to join us on our journey as we redefine the spice market with quality, innovation, and flavor.
